Tungsten (W)

Element Name:
Tungsten
Symbol:
W
Main Characteristics:
  • Tungsten is a chemical element that is a metal.
  • It is very hard and heavy.
  • Scientists are studying its properties.
  • One important property is its very high melting point.
Where it is Found:
Tungsten is found in the Earth's crust and is extracted from rocks.
Uses:
  • Because it is strong and has a high melting point, tungsten is very useful.
  • It is used in light bulb filaments.
  • It is used to make strong alloys.
Research:
Scientists continue to search for new ways to use the unique properties of this element.
